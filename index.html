Dripstone
Dripstone, the "Earth" part of Earth & Water. E&W adds a new dripstone brick set, an entire underground structure, and mob, the Bore. Not to mention Dark Dripstone and its set.
Prismarine
Prismarine, the obvious "Water" part of Earth & Water. E&W adds Prismarine Pillars, Prismarine Tiles, an Conduit themed structure, a mob known as the Brine, and the block everyone needed: the Dark Prismarine Wall.

About the Mod

Earth & Water is a comprehensive Minecraft mod that enhances the natural world with powerful earth and water mechanics. Discover new biomes, master elemental abilities, and explore ancient structures filled with mystical powers.

From terraforming landscapes to controlling the flow of rivers, Earth & Water brings a new dimension of gameplay that seamlessly integrates with vanilla Minecraft while adding depth and wonder to your adventures.
