Concept for E&W

The idea for Earth & Water began in early 2024 as a response to the lack of nature-focused mods. The goal was to blend Minecraft's elemental gaps with immersive structure design and mob behavior.

Version 1.0.0 – Foundations

The first major release introduced a comprehensive set of building blocks, items, and gameplay mechanics that established the core identity of Earth & Water.
